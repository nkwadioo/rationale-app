About

Rationale (Pty) Ltd, is an independent company operating from Pretoria, Gauteng. The company was found in May 2020. The company aims to provide established value adding engineering services to our clients in an effective, efficient and value optimized manner. It also commits to employ and maintain highly reputable skills and technology needed to meet each project’s needs.

Rationale (Pty) Ltd currently offers its services through services and has the capacity to cater to the broader SADC region on request. The company has the ability to expand on its capacity and scope by means of existing and potential MOA’s with associate companies and independent professionals.

Our vision is to realise and maintain the required symbiosis between human skills and ethics, as well as technology, to present the most effective and customer orientated service in all situations.

Mission

Our mission is to assist clients to build and maintain building infrastructure and facilities in a manner that is safe, effective for use and compliant with all applicable statutes. It also includes the display of sound project management skills at all stages of projects.
